Dane Natoli

I'm a web developer, currently living in Whistler, Canada and working as an Engineer in Residence for PayrollHero.com.

I grew up in Melbourne, Australia, always interested in computers and the web. The first thing I ever made was a Nintendo 64 cheat site I hosted on Geocities when I was 12. I continued pursuing my interest in computers, completing a Computer Science degree at RMIT in 2007.

In 2006 I began working for a small software company called HannanIT, developing software solutions for various clients around Melbourne.

After taking a year off to head to Whistler Blackcomb for the 09/10 ski season (including the Winter Olympics!), I came back to HannanIT, helping transform the software team into an awesome Agile development shop, Lambda Software where I took the role of Software Team Lead. I also worked hard to develop my web skills, becoming the tech lead on all Ruby on Rails projects.

I recently created a website called Yaktracker to track the progress made by two of my friends as they attempted to drive from London, England to Ulaanbataar, Mongolia.

If you want to know more about me and my work, feel free to check out my resume, github, Twitter and LinkedIn.

When not sitting behind a computer, I can be found either on the ski fields, in a pub, hidden behind a book or sitting on the couch with an Xbox controller in my hand.
